Welcome to the BioControl Group

We work at the intersection of biology, mathematics and computer science to deliver solutions to live in a better world.

Our research is focused on developing quantitative tools to understand cell dynamics and engineering-inspired methodologies for controlling living system functions, with the final aim to both disentangle and reprogram the complexity governing life. Her approach combines tools from different disciplines: control engineering, systems and synthetic biology, and computer science.
